About

The Basílica i Temple Expiatori de la Sagrada Família is a church in the Eixample district of Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain, and is currently the largest unfinished Catholic church in the world. Designed by the Catalan architect Antoni Gaudí, his work on the building is part of a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
